Insights

SDLC and STLC: the discipline behind reliable delivery.

The Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) provides the framework for planning, designing, building, testing, deploying, and maintaining software. The Software Testing Life Cycle (STLC) complements it by organizing testing activities from requirement analysis through test closure.

Together, they help teams reduce risk, improve quality, and deliver software with confidence. At Nexora Digital, we use both disciplines to align business goals, engineering execution, and quality assurance in a consistent, measurable way.
